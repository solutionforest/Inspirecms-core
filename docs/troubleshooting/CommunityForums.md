For technical questions, consider using Stack Overflow with appropriate tags:

## Community Guidelines

When participating in community forums, please follow these guidelines:

### Do's:

- **Search First**: Check if your question has already been answered
- **Be Specific**: Clearly describe your issue or question
- **Format Code**: Use proper code formatting in your posts
- **Show Research**: Mention what you've already tried
- **Help Others**: Share your knowledge when you can
- **Be Respectful**: Treat all community members with respect
- **Follow Up**: Mark solutions and thank helpers

### Don'ts:

- **No Spam**: Don't advertise unrelated products or services
- **No Duplicates**: Avoid posting the same question in multiple places
- **No Private Information**: Don't share API keys, passwords, or personal data
- **No Harassment**: Do not engage in personal attacks or harass other users
